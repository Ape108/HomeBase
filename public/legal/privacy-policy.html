Privacy Policy

Last updated: 11-26-2024

This privacy policy applies to the HomeBase application available at https://homebaseworkflow.com

1. Information We Collect

HomeBase ("we", "our", or "us") collects the following information:

  • Google Account information when you sign in
  • Access to Google Drive documents you explicitly share with our application
  • Workspace configurations and preferences

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide document management and workspace organization features
  • Save your workspace preferences
  • Improve our services

3. Google Drive Access

Our application:

  • Only accesses files you explicitly select
  • Cannot see or modify files you haven't shared
  • Uses Google's secure OAuth 2.0 protocol for authentication

4. Data Storage

We store:

  • Your workspace configurations
  • Document references (not the actual documents)
  • User preferences

5. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal information or document data with third parties.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Revoke access to your Google Drive
